FAQ

What types of photography do you offer?

I specialize in portrait, event, and lifestyle photography. Whether it's a wedding, family shoot, or personal branding session — I’ve got you covered.

How can I book a session?

You can use the contact form on the website or email me directly. We'll discuss your needs, availability, and location before confirming a date.

When will I receive my edited photos?

Turnaround time is typically 7–10 business days. I’ll send you a link to a private gallery where you can download the high-resolution images.
